Due Date

All submissions are due on April 7, 2023, 5 pm.

History

In March 1975, the ADCMW held their first The Real Show. The Real Show was an annual competition giving college students the opportunity to solve real-life communication design problems. The idea was simple: give “real-world” design problems to local design students for them to solve in their own way. Students take the challenge of real competition, real projects, and real deadlines.

We invite students to enter the 2023 competition. We want to see your solutions to actual problems that area designers were asked to solve in their professional practice. Entries will be judged by a distinguished jury of local designers and educators.

Free Enter

To enter, students are to choose one of the problems below and create their solution within the limitations described. Students may collaborate with another student, and enter as many projects as they wish.

All undergraduate students currently enrolled in an accredited college or university are eligible to participate.

The judges will be looking at concept, design, and execution. After judging is complete, professors will be notified as to who is in the show.

Winners

All winners included in the show will receive an Award of Merit. There will also be Gold, Silver, and Award of Excellence winners.
