Call for Entries

An Art Director’s Club of Metropolitan Washington tradition since 1975, is now at George Mason University’s School of Art

We invite design students to enter The Real Show 2023 competition. We want to see your solutions to actual problems that area designers were asked to solve in their professional practice. Entries will be judged by a distinguished jury of local designers and educators.

Free to Enter

A spring exhibition held at George Mason University's School of Art, will display the winning entries with an award ceremony honoring the Gold, Silver, and Award of Excellence winners.

Eligibility

All undergraduate students currently enrolled in an accredited college or university are eligible to participate. Part-time students working more than 1,000 hours annually in the field are not eligible.

Due Date

All submissions will be due on April 7, 2023. Details for submission coming soon.
